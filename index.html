Profile

Though being born in a very simple town like Watsonville, I discovered my passion for design and coding with the resources given to me by my classmates and teachers. I grew up in a community that inspires creativity and care for one another, that blend gave me the passion to create mediums like comic books and graphic design.


Being a gamer as well, I found to have that be more of a passion of mine when I dive deep to the UX/UI design aspect of video games and why they’re important to that experience. Great experience is key to many and why they love the medium, and it’s why I strive every day to reach that goal satisfying that experience for everyone.
